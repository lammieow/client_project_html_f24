Student Success Portal

Our mission is to empower UM graduate students by providing easy access to essential resources that support academic success, personal well-being, and crisis management. Navigate our user-friendly portal to discover everything you need to thrive on campus. We are here to help you achieve your full potential.

The Office of Student Life strives to create an inclusive and engaging campus community where all students feel they belong and can succeed.

In all that we do, our values show through and include: Compassion, Sustainability, Inclusion, Active Citizenship, Holistic Student Development, Collaboration, & Ethical Leadership

We are here for you. Begin your journey with support!

The Office of Student Success want to support you both inside and outside of the classroom.
