Student Success Portal

Our mission is to empower UM graduate students by providing easy access to essential resources that support academic success, personal well-being, and crisis management. Navigate our user-friendly portal to discover everything you need to thrive on campus. We are here to help you achieve your full potential.

The Office of Student Life strives to create an inclusive and engaging campus community where all students feel they belong and can succeed.

In all that we do, our values show through and include: Compassion, Sustainability, Inclusion, Active Citizenship, Holistic Student Development, Collaboration, & Ethical Leadership

We are here for you. Begin your journey with support!

The Office of Student Success want to support you both inside and outside of the classroom.

Academic Success

Our Academic Advising team is here to support you at every stage of your academic journey. Whether you’re exploring majors, navigating course selection, or planning your career path, we offer personalized guidance to help you make informed decisions. Our advisors work closely with students to ensure you stay on track for graduation, understand degree requirements, and maximize your university experience. Let us help you reach your full academic potential.

Physical Health

Your well-being is our top priority. University Health Services provides a range of medical care, from routine check-ups and immunizations to urgent care and wellness programs. Our dedicated healthcare professionals are here to ensure you have access to the treatment and resources needed to stay healthy while pursuing your studies. We also offer specialized services like sexual health education, nutrition counseling, and flu clinics, so you can focus on achieving your academic goals with confidence.

Mental Health

University life can be stressful, and we understand the importance of mental health in achieving academic and personal success. Our Mental Health Counseling services provide a confidential, supportive environment where students can talk through challenges, learn coping strategies, and build resilience. Whether you're dealing with stress, anxiety, depression, or personal concerns, our licensed counselors are here to offer individual and group therapy sessions, workshops, and crisis intervention to support your emotional well-being.
