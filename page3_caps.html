Counseling and Psychological Services

CAPs provide compassionate, confidential counseling to help you navigate life’s challenges. Our licensed professionals are here to listen, guide, and support your mental health journey, so you can thrive both personally and academically.

Every day, CAPS is involved in activities and services that strengthen our campus to be supportive, engaged and, ultimately, connected around student mental health.
